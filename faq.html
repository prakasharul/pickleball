FAQ

 
 
How do I join the club?

Complete the dues and waiver form online and submit with $10 online,check or by cash to PDPPC, PO Box xxxx, Florence,SC,29501

Do you Provide an ID card once I join the club?
We do not issue club membership ID cards at this time.

Does the club provide equipment?
The club supplies pickleballs for play.

I do not have a paddle. Do you rent them?

Sorry, we do not rent paddles. However there may be spare paddles available to play. Paddles are available for purchase from a variety of vendors.

I will in town for a couple of days. Where are you located?

PDPPC has 6 Outdoor dedicated Pickleball courts at Timrod Park – 400 Timrod Park dr, Florence,SC,29501 and 6 Indoor courts at Pearl Moore Center – 500 Barnes St, Florence,SC,29501.

Do you Rotate Players when the courts are full?

We have a rotation system of play si that all have equal access. We take turns playing with whomever is on the courts. Players cannot monopolize the courts for their own play when players are waiting.

 
 

The PDPPC as the club fosters pickleball in the Pee Dee region by helping facilities organize pickleball programs, teaching and promotions. The club has over 160 registered pickleball players and growing.
